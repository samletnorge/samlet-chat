Design Principles

  • Minimalism: Clean, uncluttered interfaces with clear hierarchy
  • Consistency: Reusable patterns across the platform
  • Accessibility: Sufficient color contrast and clear interactive states
  • Modern: Rounded corners (6-8px), subtle shadows, smooth transitions
  • Professional: Limited color palette, intentional use of color
